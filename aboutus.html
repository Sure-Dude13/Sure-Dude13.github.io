About us and what we do

Litter Angels is aiming to eliminate litter, end waste and improve places. This means more to us than just picking up litter. It means creating clean beaches, parks and streets. It means creating sustainable practices and eliminating unnecessary waste.

It’s about working with people, businesses, organisations and public bodies to care for the environment on our doorstep.

We fight for people’s right to live and work in places they can be proud of and prosper in.
